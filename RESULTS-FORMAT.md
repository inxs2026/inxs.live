# Racing Results Format

**Last Updated:** February 20, 2026

---

## Mandatory Format

```
🏇 [TRACK NAME] RESULTS - [DATE]

📊 TOTAL PICKS: X (Y races × 3 picks)

🏆 WINNERS: X/30 (X.X%)
📈 TOP 3 FINISHES: X/30 (X.X%)

**WINNING PICKS:**
• Race X: #X HORSE NAME (Pick #X) - [brief note]
• Race X: #X HORSE NAME (Pick #X) - [brief note]

**KEY INSIGHTS:**
[Methodology observations, what worked/didn't work]

**BOTTOM LINE:**
[Honest assessment]
```

---

## Critical Rules

### **Total Picks = Races × 3**
- 10 races = 30 picks
- 12 races = 36 picks
- 8 races = 24 picks

### **Count ALL Picks**
- Winners: How many picks finished 1st
- Top 3: How many picks finished 1st, 2nd, OR 3rd
- Both percentages use TOTAL PICKS as denominator

### **Be Honest**
- ❌ Wrong: "5 wins in 10 races (50%)"
- ✅ Right: "5/30 picks won (16.7%)"
- No spin, no misleading framing

---

## Example (10 races, 30 picks)

**If results were:**
- 5 picks won (finished 1st)
- 8 picks placed (finished 2nd)
- 7 picks showed (finished 3rd)
- 10 picks missed (finished 4th or worse)

**Correct display:**
```
📊 TOTAL PICKS: 30 (10 races × 3 picks)

🏆 WINNERS: 5/30 (16.7%)
📈 TOP 3 FINISHES: 20/30 (66.7%)
```

Because: 5 wins + 8 places + 7 shows = 20 top-3 finishes

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't say:** "5 wins in 10 races (50% win rate)"
- This makes 16.7% sound like 50%

❌ **Don't say:** "9 board finishes in 10 races (90% board rate)"
- This hides that we had 30 picks, not 10

❌ **Don't spin the numbers**
- Report facts, not marketing

---

**Purpose:** Honest, accurate performance tracking so we know if the methodology is actually working.
