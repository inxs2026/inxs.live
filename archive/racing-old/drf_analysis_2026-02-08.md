## KEY TAKEAWAYS LEARNED:

1. **Sound horses work AND race regularly** (every 2-4 weeks)
2. **Red flag = Big gaps** between races OR between workouts
3. **No workouts = hiding something** (soundness issue)
4. **Geldings after long layoffs** = often soundness surgery
5. **Claimed horses sitting** = new owner found a problem
6. **Performance + form together** = look for steady or improving Beyers
