### Beyer Interpretation (Carlo's Wisdom)
Beyers tell a good story but NOT the whole story:
- High Beyer + fast pace = legitimate speed, wired the race
- High Beyer + slow pace = inflated by easy setup
- Low Beyer + closing = might be better than number suggests if trip was bad
- Declining Beyers = horse in decline or health issue
- Sporadic Beyers = inconsistent, trip/surface dependent
- Beyers don't tell: trip trouble, equipment changes, surface shift, trainer motivation, weight/jockey changes
